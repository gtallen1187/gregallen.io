Stuff I like

Below is a list of products and services that I use on a daily basis and highly recommend. Some of these products are free, others are not.

Finance

  • Coinbase - Bitcoin wallet and online marketplace.
    • If you sign up using the above link and purchase at least $100 worth of bitcoin, we both receive a $10 bonus.
  • Digit.co - Automatic savings tool. Studies your spending habits and incrementally deducts small amounts of money at times when it knows you won't notice, and puts it in a savings account for you.
    • If you sign up using the above link, we both receive a $5 bonus.
  • Simple - Online banking.
  • BitMaker - Earn free bitcoin for watching 5-second ads.
    • Use code MOQD4T and we both receive a 0.0000125 BTC bonus.
  • Plastc - Credit card that allows you to store all of your credit cards, loyalty cards, etc in a single card. An improvement over the disappointing Coin.

Tech
